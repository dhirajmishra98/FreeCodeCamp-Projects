Shushan Singh Rajput

The actor who ruled bollywood

Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor known for his work in Hindi cinema. Rajput started his career after dropping out of his engineering course at the Delhi College of Engineering and entering the theatre industry in Mumbai.


Here's time line of Shushant Singh Rajput's Life :

  • 2006-2011 : Early career and television.

  • 2011-2015 : Film debut and Yash Raj Films.

  • 2015-2018 : Dhoni biopic, Raabta and Kedarnath.

  • 2018-2020 : Enterpreneurial debut and later career.
